# 📘 Statistics & Probability Quick Revision Notes

A collection of concise **Statistics & Probability** revision notes shared by **Dr. Ronald Bbosa** and published with his permission.

These notes are intended to serve as a **quick reference** for students preparing for coursework, assignments, quizzes, and examinations. They are designed to complement lectures and textbooks, not replace them.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This repository aims to:

- Provide a fast way to revise key statistical and probability concepts.
- Help students review important formulas and definitions.
- Offer an organized collection of easy-to-reference revision sheets.
